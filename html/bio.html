I started my career in Customer Success logging hundreds of hours of in-person interviews and strategy sessions with B2B users. I've managed teams, projects, and most recently been invovled in CX strategy, leading user research initiatives and measuring customer outcomes.



I believe that “good UX” is a practice as much as it is a result. Designers are responsible for ensuring that users are getting real value from the product, with minimal friction, in a way that minimizes client attrition, and they have to do so while impacting revenue for the company, because without that, there is no experience to speak of.
