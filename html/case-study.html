BROG

Bróg (“brogue”), a mobile app for language-learning, interconnects a network of global users, focusing on a narrow goal of improving their foreign language accents. Users submit daily pronunciation videos for review, and in turn review others, while earning badges for their progress.

The Challenge

There are many apps that focus on language learning. Some provide lessons in grammar and vocabulary, some connect users for open-ended communication, and others focus on pronunciation with voice recognition software. They each have their shortcomings, from a lack of pronunciation focus, to a lack of human touch. What if you really wanted to focus on pronunciation and wanted feedback from real native speakers? No apps on the market are tuned to accommodating this need.

The Solution

Bróg presents a unique offering, combining three specific features in a unique recipe for success: (1) a narrow focus on pronunciation, (2) network of users for direct human interaction, and (3) a usage/learning model for quick, daily use.
